## 전쟁 전 (2019~2022.2.23)

### Z-2 — 2021.04.02 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Spoke with @POTUS. The U.S. is our strategic partner and supporter of our sovereignty. We discussed the escalation in Donbas."

### Z-3 — 2022.01.28 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "I want to address the people of Ukraine. There is a lot of information in the media about a possible large-scale war. We are not afraid."

### Z-4 — 2022.02.23 | @ZelenskyyUa

## 전쟁 직후 (2022.2.24~3월)

### Z-5 — 2022.02.24 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Russia has attacked Ukraine. Putin has begun a war against Ukraine, against the entire democratic world. He wants to destroy my country, our country, everything we've built, everything we live for."

### Z-6 — 2022.02.25 | @ZelenskyyUa (셀피 영상)
> "Good evening, everyone. The leader of the faction is here. The head of the President's Office is here. Prime Minister Shmyhal is here. Podolyak is here. The president is here. We are all here. Our soldiers are here. The citizens are here. We are all here defending our independence, our country. And we will continue to do so. Glory to our defenders. Glory to our women defenders. Glory to Ukraine."

### Z-7 — 2022.02.26 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "I asked 27 leaders of Europe whether Ukraine will be in NATO. I asked directly. Not everyone answered. But we are not afraid. We are not afraid of anything."

### Z-8 — 2022.02.26 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "The fight is here; I need ammunition, not a ride."

### Z-9 — 2022.02.28 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Ukraine has officially applied for EU membership. We ask for immediate accession under a new special procedure."

### Z-10 — 2022.03.01 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Last night was brutal. Rocket attacks on Kharkiv. This is the price of freedom. We will not give up and we will not lose."

### Z-11 — 2022.03.01 | @ZelenskyyUa (유럽의회 연설 후)
> "Nobody is going to break us. We are strong. We are Ukrainians. We have a desire to see our children alive. I think it's a fair one."

### Z-12 — 2022.03.08 | @ZelenskyyUa (영국 의회 연설 후)
> "We will fight till the end, at sea, in the air. We will fight for our land, whatever the cost. We will fight in the forests, in the fields, on the shores, in the streets."

### Z-13 — 2022.03.16 | @ZelenskyyUa (미국 의회 연설 후)
> "I have a dream. I have a need. I need to protect our sky. I need your decision, your help, which means exactly the same — the same you feel when you hear the words: 'I have a dream.'"

### Z-14 — 2022.03.16 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "To all the people of the United States: I call on you to remember September 11th. Remember how the sky was dark. Remember the fear. That is what Ukraine lives every day."

## 전쟁 장기화 (2022.4~2023)

### Z-15 — 2022.04.04 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "The world has seen what Russian soldiers did in Bucha. Intentional killing, torture, rape. They are not soldiers. They are butchers."

### Z-16 — 2022.06.10 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "100+ days of full-scale war. We have already returned more than 1000 of our people from captivity. We will return everyone."

### Z-17 — 2022.08.24 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Today Ukraine celebrates Independence Day. We will not congratulate each other as usual. We will not wish each other a 'happy holiday.' Because there is nothing happy about bombs and occupation. But we will say: Happy Birthday, Ukraine. We will not let you die."

### Z-18 — 2022.09.21 | @ZelenskyyUa (UN 총회)
> "A crime has been committed against Ukraine, and we demand just punishment. Ukraine wants peace. Europe wants peace. The world wants peace. And we have seen who is the only one who wants war."

### Z-19 — 2022.11.11 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Kherson. Ours. Unconquered. And free."

### Z-20 — 2022.12.21 | @ZelenskyyUa
> "Thank you America, thank you for your support, thank you for this visit. Thank you @POTUS, Congress, and the American people. Ukraine needs just and lasting peace, and we are working exactly for that."

### Z-21 — 2023.09.19 | @ZelenskyyUa (UN 총회)
> "Addressed the UN General Assembly. The world can stop Russia. Weapons are needed. Not just words."
